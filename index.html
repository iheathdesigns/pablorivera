About me

Photographer, drawer, watercolorist and sculptor

I was born in New York City, graduated from Cooper Union, furthered my education at The Education Alliance, The School of Visual Arts as well as classes at University of North Florida and Florida State College, Jacksonville. Workshops with Charles Reid, Don Andrews, Robert Leedy and Mary Whyte, added to my painting education. My passion for art in all its facets is what I am all about. I have exhibited in New Jersey, New York, Connecticut, Rhode Island, California and Florida. In Jacksonville, Florida, I have exhibited with the St. Augustine Art Association, The Museum of Contemporary Art, Jacksonville, The Cummer Museum of Art and Gardens, The Cultural Center at Ponte Vedra Beach, Art After Dark, The Art Center Cooperative Inc., Jacksonville, The Coalition of Visual Arts, The Adele Grage Cultural Center in Atlantic Beach and The Southlight Gallery.
